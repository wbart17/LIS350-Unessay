ANDREW MCCLOUD

Andrew McCloud is currently a Senior Front End Engineer working at Abstract. At the age of 18, McCloud created Splicd. Splicd gave users of Youtube the ability to generate a link to a specified time on a Youtube video. Nowadays, the feature is offically implemented, but McCloud was the first to bring forth the feature.

Although, this feature seems small, it gave many users some extra quality of life when using Youtube. What seems like a small feature now possibly led to the newer feature we see today such as the timestamp feature many Youtube videos share, allowing users to easily get to the more important parts of the videos.
