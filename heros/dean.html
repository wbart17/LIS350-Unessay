MARK DEAN

Mark Dean is a computer scientist and engineer who helped develop a number of technologies for IBM.

Some of the most impactful include the color PC monitor and the first gigahertz chip. He is the first African American named an IBM fellow in 1996.

He was inducted into the National Inventors Hall of Fame in 1997.
