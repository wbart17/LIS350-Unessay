What is this About

Two of us, Justin and Sreeram, had an interview over Zoom introducing ourselves and talking about people of color in tech that recently inspired us. William joined our group for this project as he was unable to find a partner for assignment #1. Often times the spotlight does not shine on people who have very inspirational stories and are people of color.

During the interview we talked about how one does not have to reinvent the wheel in regards to technology to better peoples lives. Sometimes it is just about adding some quality of live for the people using the technology.

Some issues we ran into for the interview were some camera issues leading to a less personal interview as one person could not have their camera on. Additionally, it was difficult to find some in depth information for some of the POC heros as they are not constantly interview like some of the more well known leaders in tech.
